Rock, paper & scissors

Rules of the game;

Rock paper scissors is a school yard classic. Each player to the count of three thrusts a closed fist whilst chanting "rock, paper, scissors" before revealing their choice on the third thrust.
The player who wins is the one with the dominant hand.

The order goes;

  1. Rock beats Scissors
  2. Paper beats Rock
  3. Scissors beats Paper
